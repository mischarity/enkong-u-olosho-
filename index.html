Enkong’u Olosho Youth Initiative

"A Voice of Hope from the Heart of Maasailand"

About Us

Enkong’u Olosho is a grassroots youth-led initiative based in Mashuru, Kajiado County in Maasai land. Founded in 2024, we support the needy and vulnerable through compassion, community action, and sustainable solutions.

About Mashuru

Mashuru sits along the Emali–Loitokitok Road, roughly 100 km southeast of Nairobi. The area is semi-arid, home to around 95,000+ people—predominantly Maasai pastoralists practicing livestock rearing and small-scale farming.

Mission & Vision

Our vision: A united, empowered Maasai community where no one is left behind — especially widows, orphans, and persons with disabilities.

  • Community-based fundraising
  • Food and clothing drives
  • Youth empowerment workshops
  • Livelihood skill training
  • Emergency and home-based support

Impact Since 2024

  • Reached over 180 families with direct support
  • Empowered 30+ youth volunteers
  • Held 3 successful fundraising events
  • Partnered with community leaders and elders
  • Raised awareness about poverty, drought and unity in Maasailand

Through our actions, many families have been lifted from extreme poverty, children have returned to school, and we've established ourselves as a model of youth‑driven development.

In Collaboration With

We work in alignment with the leadership of Governor Joseph Ole Lenku (Kajiado County), whose support has helped increase accountability and reach.

Current Focus

  • Building an emergency food bank
  • Launching a girls’ mentorship program
  • Providing dignity kits and clothing
  • Mobilizing more youth volunteers
